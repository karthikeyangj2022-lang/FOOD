About Us

“Where Sophistication Meets Flavor”

Welcome to Tomato Restaurant, an elevated dining destination where timeless elegance and contemporary cuisine come together in perfect harmony. More than a restaurant, Tomato is a celebration of refined taste, artistry, and culinary excellence. At the heart of Tomato is Executive Chef Alessandro Vitale, a globally trained culinary visionary with over 20 years of experience in some of the world’s most celebrated Michelin-starred kitchens. Chef Vitale brings a masterful touch to every plate, blending classical European techniques with seasonal, locally sourced ingredients to create dishes that are both bold and beautifully balanced.

At Tomato, dining is not just a meal—it’s a journey led by culinary passion.

About Chef

At Tomato Restaurant, Executive Chef Marco Rossi redefines Italian fine dining with refined simplicity and exceptional craftsmanship. Trained in Naples and Paris, Chef Marco brings over 15 years of experience to the kitchen, transforming the humble tomato into a centerpiece of culinary artistry. Each dish reflects his precision, creativity, and respect for seasonality — from delicate tomato consommés to hand-crafted pasta enriched with sun-ripened heirloom varieties. Chef Marco’s philosophy is rooted in balance: tradition meets innovation, rustic flavors meet elegant presentation. Under his direction, Tomato Restaurant has become a celebration of flavor and form — where every plate tells a story of passion, heritage, and the timeless beauty of Italian cuisine.
